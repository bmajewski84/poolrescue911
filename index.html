About Ben

Not Your Average Pool Guy.

El Dorado Pool Rescue was founded by Ben Majewski — an Army Veteran, Firefighter, and certified HazMat Specialist serving El Dorado Hills. Ben discovered a passion for pool chemistry as a way to decompress from the demands of public safety work.

What started as helping friends troubleshoot problem pools grew into a professional service built on the same principles Ben applies in emergency response: precision, accountability, and doing the job right the first time.

Every pool gets treated with the Langelier Saturation Index — the same standard used by commercial aquatic facilities. You get detailed weekly photo reports, honest itemized billing, and a technician who genuinely cares.
